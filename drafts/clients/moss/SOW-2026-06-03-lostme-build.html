1. Scope of work

Phase 1 (collaborative, approximately 22 hours over 2 weeks)

  • Audit of the existing LostMe build and user flows.
  • UX redesign delivering wireframes and a shared design system, run collaboratively via video call.
  • Data model and Supabase schema specification migrating from the current MongoDB setup.
  • Native iOS and Android build scoping covering state, authentication, and offline sync.

Phase 1 deliverables: wireframes, refined product specification, reworked repository skeletons, and a fixed Phase 2 commencement date.

Phase 2 (build, approximately 2 to 3 weeks)

  • Native iOS build (Swift / SwiftUI).
  • Native Android build (Kotlin / Compose).
  • Shared design system across both platforms.
  • Supabase migration covering schema, data, authentication, and Row Level Security.
  • API layer and backend wiring.
  • Push notifications and offline sync.
  • App Store and Play Store submission packages including screenshots, listings, privacy questionnaires, and content ratings.
  • QA, polish passes, debugging.
  • Publishing to the SeedTree Earth Apple Developer and Google Play accounts.

4. Out-of-scope work and overflow

Anything outside the named scope (additional features, third-party integrations beyond those listed, redesign of work already delivered, support for additional platforms) is billed at $80/hr ex GST in 30-minute increments, invoiced at the end of the calendar month in which the work was performed. Out-of-scope requests are confirmed in writing (email is sufficient) before work begins.

5. Optional post-launch retainer

On request from SeedTree Earth, Ecodia will provide a monthly maintenance retainer at $100/mo ex GST ($110/mo incl GST), commencing on the later of the Apple App Store live date and the Google Play Console live date. The retainer covers, with no monthly hours ceiling:

  • Operating system update compatibility patches as Apple and Google release iOS and Android major versions.
  • Dependency upgrades and security patches.
  • Store re-submissions when Apple or Google force version bumps.
  • Small bug fixes reported by SeedTree Earth or surfaced through store review.

Most calendar months are expected to require little or no work; the retainer is structured as continuity cover rather than a metered allowance. Work outside the maintenance category (new features, redesign of shipped functionality, additional platforms, scope expansion) is billed at the $80/hr ex GST overflow rate per section 4. The retainer is cancellable by either side on #30 days written notice. Without an active retainer, post-launch work from SeedTree Earth is billed ad-hoc at $80/hr ex GST.

6. Term and termination

This SOW takes effect on signed acceptance and runs until completion of Phase 2 (and any active retainer that follows). Either party may terminate on #7 days written notice. On termination during an active build phase, Ecodia invoices for work in progress through the termination date at the $80/hr ex GST overflow rate against the deposit balance; any unspent portion of the deposit is refunded to SeedTree Earth within 14 days; any spent portion above the deposit is invoiced and due within 7 days.

7. Confidentiality

Each party will treat the other's non-public business and technical information as confidential and will not disclose it without consent, save as required by law. This obligation survives termination.
