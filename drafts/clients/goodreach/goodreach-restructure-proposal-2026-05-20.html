Goodreach

The setup

From Tate and Ecodia. For Tom, Kurt, Angelica. 20 May 2026.

Goodreach is a SaaS platform of lightweight AI tools for not-for-profits and small consultancies. Each tool draws on the organisation's own documents, so every answer comes out of their own material. Simple to start, easy to refer in a sentence.

What it solves

Staff are already using AI at work, usually by pasting documents into consumer chatbots that keep the data and know nothing about how the organisation runs. That leaks sensitive information and produces sloppy, inconsistent work. Goodreach is the sanctioned version: one place where staff use AI on the organisation's own material, with zero friction, and every output holds to the same standard.

The tools

The tools are the product. Each one is repeatable across customers and lightly customised per organisation (their documents, their templates, their terminology).

  • Meeting capture and minutes
  • Ask-your-documents, the org's own files as a knowledge base
  • Governance and ACNC health checks
  • Board report generator
  • Grant application drafter

More tools follow the same shape as customers ask.

Built for trust

The data stays in Australia and is never used to train a model. Each organisation's information is walled off from every other. The easy channel is also the safe one, which is what gets staff to use it instead of going around it.
