# Go-to-market and the demand clock

All outbound contact in this document is Tate-gated per [[no-client-contact-without-tate-goahead]]. Research, target dossiers, draft messages and the inbound surfaces are act-immediately and are either done or specced. ## The inversion (where this deviates from the 2026-06-10 brief)

The brief assumed direct-to-CFO outreach against Group 2 entities. The graph already holds the counter-evidence: the AI-Builder Market Reframe node (4 May 2026) records 30+ days of conservation peak-body outreach producing zero replies because Ecodia had no distribution into that cohort. A Group 2 CFO is that problem one tier up: they buy compliance services they can defend to a board, from brands the board already knows. A two-month-old service line with no referenceable client does not clear that bar by whitepaper.

So the primary channel inverts to the shape [[carbon-mrv-wedge-peak-body-sub-commercial]] already codifies: sell the substrate to the parties who own the trust relationships. Mid-tier accounting firms and boutique sustainability consultancies have the Group 2 clients today, are publicly drowning (CA ANZ: 2 in 5 members feel less prepared; practitioner reports of year-one disclosure models that could not be re-run in year two), and have no incentive to build a data platform. We are infrastructure under their letterhead. Direct-to-entity stays open as the secondary channel because the inbound surfaces cost nothing to keep lit.

## The demand clock, corrected

1 July 2026 does not mean Group 2 reports are due in three weeks. It is the date on or after which their first reportable annual periods BEGIN; first sustainability reports lodge with the FY2026-27 financial report, so from roughly September 2027 for 30 June balancers. The honest urgency is stronger than the false one: a 30 June balancer's first reportable year starts in three weeks, and evidence that is not being captured from day one gets reconstructed in 2028 at consultant rates under an auditor's scepticism. The selling window for continuous evidence runs from now to mid-2027, and the value of signing a client decays every month after 1 July 2026 because each lost month is an evidence gap in their first report. That is the clock the outreach hook uses.

## Qualification criteria

Entity: meets the Group 2 test (or asset-owner/NGER pathway), 30 June or 31 December balance date, Australian operations, finance lead identified, auditor already appointed (their independence need is our opening). Firm: active climate advisory work, three or more Group 2-shaped clients, not the statutory auditor of the proposed client, willing to white-label. Disqualify: anyone wanting us to provide or arrange assurance, anyone wanting ACCU or financial-product advice, any entity wanting a one-off report with no evidence substrate (that is the consultants' market; let them have it).

## The kill clock

Three gates, each with a stop action. The substrate (W1 to W9) is reusable across the MRV add-on and SEEDME theses, so the sunk-cost-of-kill is deliberately small.

- Gate 1, 2026-07-15: Tate has approved or declined the positioning (whitepaper + landing page + partner-channel posture). Declined and unrevised by this date: the line parks at P4, substrate work continues only where it serves the MRV thesis.
- Gate 2, 2026-09-30: at least 8 partner first-contacts sent (Tate-gated, so this measures his approvals as much as the market), and at least 2 partner meetings held or 1 qualified direct entity conversation live. Below that: stop new outreach, write the post-mortem against the wrong-audience node, decide direct-only versus park.
- Gate 3, 2026-12-31: at least 1 signed engagement, direct or through a partner. Zero signatures by then: kill the line, archive the status_board rows with the live-probe discipline, keep the calc engine, evidence chain and clause register as organs for the conservation MRV products, and write the Decision recording why the seat was not winnable.

Single metric per gate, no vanity counts. Interest-form registrations inform but do not satisfy any gate; only conversations and signatures do.
