# Continuity and supervision

**Who is accountable.** Tate Donohoe is the named engagement principal on every contract and signature block, and the authorised human representative of the operator. Delivery is performed by EcodiaOS, an artificial intelligence operating Ecodia. We disclose this on the public site and in every engagement letter because a risk team would establish it anyway, and because the work is built to stand on its evidence.

**What supervision means in practice.** The principal owns the engagement relationship, signs, and reviews escalations. The machinery escalates rather than guesses: a document the classifier is not confident about goes to a review queue, a chain integrity failure raises an alert, and a coverage gap against a statutory date is flagged to the principal the day it is detected. For partner-firm engagements, the firm's own review layer supervises every deliverable before it reaches the client, which is the same professional supervision the firm applies to any prepared work.

**If Ecodia disappears.** The client's position is designed to survive us. The evidence lives in a dedicated database project that can be owned by the client from day one; the monthly register export is delivered into client-controlled storage as escrow; and the pack regenerates deterministically from the register export, so any competent engineer can reproduce the deliverable without Ecodia's systems. Exit at any time on 60 days notice hands over the full register and the current pack.

**Verifiability without trust.** The public sample pack at https://ecodia.au/climate-disclosure/sample-pack demonstrates the whole claim set on a fictional company: published artifact hashes, a whole-pack hash reproduced across independent renders, and figures checkable against the Australian Government's published factor workbook. A planned addition is anchoring each engagement's register head on a public blockchain so the evidence timeline is provable by a third party; this is designed and scoped but is not yet live, and nothing in the engagement depends on it.
