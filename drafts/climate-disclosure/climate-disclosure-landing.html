The evidence layer for climate disclosure.

From 1 July 2026, Australia's Group 2 reporting entities begin their first reportable years under AASB S2. The report is due after the year ends. The evidence has to exist from the day it begins. It cannot be reconstructed afterwards, and every auditor knows it.

what it is

A continuous evidence register underneath your sustainability report. Every invoice, meter read and fuel record is captured as it happens, hash-chained so it cannot be quietly rewritten, and carried through to a Scope 1 and 2 baseline where every disclosed tonne traces back to its source documents and the factor tables that produced it. Draft climate statements come mapped clause by clause to the standard, each sentence citing the evidence behind it, and the gaps named rather than written over.

what it is not

Ecodia is not an auditor and does not provide assurance. Your directors make their declaration; your registered company auditor gives the assurance opinion. What we hand them is an evidence pack their testing can run against. The boundary is the product.
