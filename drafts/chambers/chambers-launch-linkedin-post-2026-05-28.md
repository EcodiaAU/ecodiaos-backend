A couple weeks back I was chatting with Matt Barmentloo, who runs the Sunshine Coast Young Chamber of Commerce and is one of those people who throws out an idea a minute. One of them was an app that Chambers of Commerce could use for... everything. I liked that one. 3 weeks later I can say the "Chambers" app is officially open for early adoption.

I researched and picked apart the problems in as many chamber-related platforms as I could find, found what people liked, what they wanted improved or added, and added a bit of my own systems and automation knowledge. I'm pretty happy with how it turned out, and I hope you are too.
