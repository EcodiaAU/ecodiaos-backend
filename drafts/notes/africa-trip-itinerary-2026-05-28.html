Vic + Cape Town coordination windows

Two Cape Town stops, both in the public-itinerary window.

13-15 Nov in Cape Town (post-Namibia overland, pre-Kruger). Short two-night window before flying out to Joburg morning of 16 Nov.

4-11 Dec in Cape Town (post-Vic Falls, pre-Kili). Full free week. This is the natural window for any extended coordination, day trips, dinners.

Vic Falls 1-4 Dec. 3 nights on the Zimbabwe side (bigger view; Devil's Pool is seasonal Zambia-side). If anyone is meeting at Vic Falls, this is the window.

Kili climb 11-18 Dec. Solo climb on a group tour. If anyone is climbing alongside, they need to be on the same operator booking + on the same start date.

VisaKAZA UniVisa $50 USD covers both Zimbabwe and Zambia (multi-entry, 30 days).

Park entryZimbabwe ~$30-50 USD. Zambia ~$20-30 USD.

Hero activityHelicopter "Flight of Angels". 12-15 min ~$150-165 USD pp + $15 park fee. 25-30 min ~$285 USD pp.

OtherSunset Zambezi cruise $65-80. Zipline $60. Bridge swing $120. Half-day rafting $160. Full-day rafting $185.

Leg 6. Kilimanjaro Climb
11 to 18 Dec 2026. 7-day Machame route.

StatusNOT YET BOOKED. Operator pick is the key open item. Lock by end August.

Route7-day Machame. Most popular + scenic + decent acclimatisation profile.

Realistic budget~$3,000 AUD mid-tier Tanzanian operator. Add $300-400 AUD crew tips (mandatory cash. Guide + 2 assistant guides + ~10 porters).

Shortlist to investigate
1. Climb Kili. KPAP-certified, 98.8% summit rate.
2. Duma Explorer. KPAP founding partner, WFR-certified guides.
3. Climbing Kilimanjaro. TripAdvisor #1, ~$2,800 USD listed.
4. Mid-tier Moshi-based with 4.8+ on SafariBookings (Kwesa Tours, Wings of Africa).

Critical checksKPAP membership (porter ethics). Summit success rate >=85%. Twice-daily pulse oximeter. Bottled oxygen on hand. Guide WFR-certified. Recent (12mo) reviews.

Standard 7-day Machame profile
Day 1: Machame Gate (1,800m) to Machame Camp (3,010m). 5-7hr hike through rainforest.
Day 2: Machame Camp to Shira Camp (3,840m). 4-6hr.
Day 3: Shira to Lava Tower (4,630m) to Barranco Camp (3,950m). Acclimatisation day.
Day 4: Barranco Wall to Karanga Camp (3,995m). Short day.
Day 5: Karanga to Barafu Camp (4,673m). 4-5hr. Early sleep.
Day 6: Summit day. Midnight start. Uhuru Peak (5,895m) at sunrise. Descend to Mweka Camp (3,068m). 12-16hr total.
Day 7: Mweka Camp to Mweka Gate (1,640m). 3-4hr descent + transfer to Moshi.

Return. JRO to BNE
18 Dec 2026. Qatar via Doha.

CarrierQatar Airways 1-stop via DOH is the only one-stop option. Otherwise 2-stop via Nairobi + Dubai or via Addis Ababa.

Duration~28 hours total with one stop.

Booking strategyPeak Southern Hemisphere summer + Christmas migration window. Book by ~5 Nov to lock $1,700-1,900 AUD. Pushing past mid-Nov risks $2,400-3,100+ in fare creep.
