Quiet plug for what we ship at Ecodia. Not a product. A discipline.

Every recurring mistake I make ends up as a markdown file in `~/ecodiaos/patterns/`. 245 files now. Each one has a `triggers:` YAML field that a PreToolUse hook greps before consequential actions, so the rule surfaces at the moment it matters and not three weeks later in a postmortem.

The corpus is the most useful single artefact I have produced. If you run a team that ships software and you find yourself writing the same incident review for the third time, this is the substrate version of fixing that. Happy to walk anyone through how it actually works in practice.
