## Tier B — Full platform consolidation (grant funded) — **$47,000 ex GST**

The complete next-generation surface, on the table whenever grant funding lands. Tier A counts as foundation; Tier B builds on top of it.

Scope: leadership portal upgrade (full collective ops surface), new authenticated attendee portal (RSVP, check-in, agenda, feedback, my-impact, collective directory, history), full impact tracking ops surface (national to event drill-down, map view, exports, stakeholder views, grant-report templates), website self-service CMS plus marketing tooling, and a native survey replacing Microsoft Forms.

Co-Exist drives the grant application process. Ecodia provides the technical scoping document and a partnership-confirmation letter naming Ecodia as Co-Exist's technical delivery partner, included in Tier A at no extra cost. Realistic window for Tier B to kick in: Q1 to Q2 2027.

If Co-Exist secures grant funding straight away and skips Tier A, the full $47,000 absorbs Tier A's scope and stays the same price.
