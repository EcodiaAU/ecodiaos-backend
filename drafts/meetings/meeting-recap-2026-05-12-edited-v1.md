## The Strategy in One Line

Wild Mountains becomes a three-layer social enterprise: a flagship leadership program at its core, community events as the feeder, and venue hire as the revenue engine — with a new brand "Wild Mountains Experiences" to attract the next generation.

---

## The Three-Layer Model

- **Layer 1 - Leadership Program (core mission):** 4-6 week immersive program for 5-6 young adults. Two capstone projects: a conservation project and a community event. Tapering supervision. Philanthropically funded (~$1,200 participant fee, ~$3,200 cost).
- **Layer 2 - Community Events (feeder):** Music weekends, plant-and-dance, film nights, women's campouts. Subcontracted to young organizers (Keely, Adam, others). $80-150 per person. 40-60% revenue split.
- **Layer 3 - Venue Hire / Corporate (revenue):** Existing, to be grown.
