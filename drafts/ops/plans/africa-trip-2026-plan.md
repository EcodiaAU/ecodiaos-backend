### Leg 5. Victoria Falls Stopover (1 to 4 Dec)

- Side. Zimbabwe (VFA) vs Zambia (LVI). Both viable. Zimbabwe side has bigger view. Zambia side has Devil's Pool (seasonal, usually low-water).
- Visa. KAZA UniVisa $50 USD covers both Zimbabwe plus Zambia (multi-entry, 30 days).
- Park entry. Zimbabwe ~$30 to 50 USD. Zambia ~$20 to 30 USD.
- Mid-range accom. $80 to 120 USD per night (Shearwater Explorers Village, Pioneers Lodge, Ilala Lodge entry tier).
- Hero activity. Helicopter "Flight of Angels". 12 to 15 min at ~$150 to 165 USD pp plus $15 park fee. 25 to 30 min at ~$285 USD pp.
- Other activities. Sunset Zambezi cruise $65 to 80, zipline $60, bridge swing $120, half-day rafting $160, full-day rafting $185.
- Budget posture. 3 nights mid-range accom (~$300 USD) plus helicopter (~$180 USD) plus visa plus park plus 2 dinners plus 1 cruise. ~$900 to 1,500 AUD.
- Status. Tate is considering cutting this leg in budget mode.

- Reputable shortlist to investigate.
  1. **Climb Kili**. KPAP-certified, 98.8% summit rate, US-facing pricing.
  2. **Duma Explorer**. KPAP founding partner, WFR-certified guides.

  - KPAP membership (ethical porter treatment).
  - Summit success rate 85% or better.

  - Bottled oxygen on hand.

  - Recent (last 12 months) TripAdvisor or SafariBookings reviews.
- Realistic budget. ~$3,000 AUD for a mid-tier reputable operator. Add ~$300 to 400 AUD for crew tips (mandatory cash. Guide plus 2 assistants plus ~10 porters).
