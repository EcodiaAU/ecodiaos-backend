# Chambers competitive landscape: read this before any chambers strategic claim

## What is true on 2026-05-21

The Sunshine Coast chamber-platform market has incumbents. Do not pitch chambers app as "first AI chamber platform" or "no competition." There are two AU-local players, two global ones, and ChamberMaster (US) has already shipped AI-powered newsletters.

| Platform | Origin | Position | Mobile? |
|---|---|---|---|
| WaveCRM (by SafeCo, QLD) | AU-local | Dominant on Sunny Coast. 50+ chamber customers across QLD including Brisbane Junior, Gold Coast, Kawana, Caloundra, Logan, Nambour, Tourism Noosa. $278.25/mo + GST flat. Marketing roadmap silent since Q3 2023. | Web-only |
| Member Jungle | AU | Multi-vertical (chambers + clubs + associations). $109-$799/mo tiered. Bigger AU competitor than Wave for broader member-org positioning. | Member-facing mobile app |
| ChamberMaster (GrowthZone) | US | Chamber-specific, global. AI-Powered Newsletter already shipped. Marketing automation already shipped. $200+/mo. | Limited |
| Chamber Nation | US | Chamber-specific, AI-marketing focused. 250+ chambers. Acquired by Valsoft April 2026. | Branded mobile app |

## Sunshine Coast Chamber Alliance (SCCA)

**Closed in 2023.** Do not propose targeting SCCA as a federation channel into individual chambers. The 14 Sunny Coast chambers operate without an active umbrella body. Sunshine Coast Council's canonical chambers page lists all 14 with direct contacts (per levelup.sunshinecoast.qld.gov.au/business-upgrades/networks-and-support/chambers-of-commerce).

## The defensible space we actually occupy

1. AU-local + AU-data-sovereign vs US ChamberMaster + Chamber Nation
2. AI-native built into the core vs Member Jungle + WaveCRM who have not deeply integrated AI yet at the AU level
3. Native mobile-first via Capacitor vs WaveCRM (web-only) and most others
4. Multi-vertical-capable from line 1 vs WaveCRM (chambers-only)
5. Self-serve tenant onboarding vs almost everyone who needs a sales call
6. AI-native build velocity (10-20x most incumbents) because we are using Claude Code, Cursor, Factory

## What WaveCRM has that we do not (yet)

- EDM with segmentation + drip campaigns
- Two-way Xero integration (chambers care about this)
- Paid event ticketing with dietary + voucher options
- Direct debit (BECS) facility
- 50+ chamber customers as social proof
- Six years of chamber-specific feature maturity

Phase B of the build sequence (chambers-feature-plan-2026-05-21.md) closes the first four gaps. The reference customers and feature maturity follow once we sign chambers.
