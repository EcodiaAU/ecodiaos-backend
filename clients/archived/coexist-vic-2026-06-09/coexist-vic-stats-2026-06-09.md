# Co-Exist Victoria - growth and retention

Pulled 9 June 2026. Sources: Co-Exist app, Eventbrite, Microsoft Forms sign-in sheet, Events & Impact 23-26 workbook.

## Growth by year

| Year | Collectives running | Events held | People who attended |
|------|--------------------:|------------:|--------------------:|
| 2024 | 1 (Melbourne) | 10 | 162 |
| 2025 | 1 (Melbourne) | 19 | 368 |
| 2026 to 9 June | 4 | 31 done, 14 booked | 486 |
| **Total** | **4** | **60 done** | **1,016** |

Geelong, Mornington Peninsula and North East Victoria all launched between February and April 2026.

## Retention by cohort

Of the 1,016 total attendees, 405 have a name and email on file. The other 611 came before sign-in was running or did not fill it in. Of the 405 on file, 111 have come back to a second Co-Exist event.

| When they first attended a Victoria event | Attendees | Came back |

| **All 405** | **405** | **27%** |

## On the data quality

The Co-Exist app only turned on for events from April / May 2026 onward. Sign-in capture has always been patchy. The real retention number is almost certainly much higher than 27%, and the real total unique audience is much higher than 405, but neither can be recovered without making it up.
